Mega

We believe that meeting inspiring people and creating a kick-ass network is one of the most valuable part of any Startup Weekend. Therefore we decided to organise the biggest Startup Weekend in Europe to make sure that all of our participants will have an extraordinary experience.

Imagine it like 4 parallel Startup Weekends running at the same venue. Each tracks will have their own topics

Smart Solutions | Health | Media | Services

Each having their own Facilitators. There will be space for 100 participants in each Tracks, and we`ll make sure that you will have the chance to meet participants from other tracks. This startup weekend is for everyone!

Tracks

Smart

Are you passionate about solving big problems with small computers? Join the Smart Revolution! Smart Track means we will be focusing the ideas we select on solving problems in the space of IoT, Smart Cities and Clean Tech. With the explosion and growing number of internet enabled devices for just about any realm of life, this edition will be as broad as your imagination allows and focused on addressing the problems and opportunities related to these devices.

Health

Are you interested in changing the way we help and care about people? We face countless numbers of challenges such as the aging society and hospitals running out of capacity. In the Health Track we will create a rare friction between business people, designers, doctors and developers to bring in new technologies which have potential of improving the healthcare system and draw attention to the new areas of Health Tech and Life Science.

Media

Do you have an idea of how to deliver our sports, music, news, movies, TV, and gaming in a more meaningful and engaging way? Or maybe these industries could benefit from today's technology? Join us in developing new and fresh ideas centered around the viewing and creation of our Media.

Services

Do you also believe that multiple areas of the service industry are ripe for disruption? You always wanted to change the way we do financial services like banking? Or you think there are ways to improve how we do insurances? Join the Services tech to explore and create new solutions in fields like Fintech, Law tech, Insurance Tech and SaaS.
