Startup Mega is an annual celebration of newly funded startups and amazing people behind the teams. It is a 3 year program that is taking place in 2017, 2018 and 2019 during the Global Entrepreneurship Week in November. We want to inspire the new generation of entrepreneurs. Startup Mega is a week long program packed with action-oriented series of events, webinars, talks and a weekend long workshop to help attendees launch a startup and build a startup in Denmark. We are kicking off in November 2017 in parallel with Global Entrepreneurship Week of Denmark. We are bringing the Danish startup community together for a week and finishing with a weekend long workshop. This is what we call the lighthouse, ensuring that people can see it from afar, and openly inviting attendees from various background to join.
